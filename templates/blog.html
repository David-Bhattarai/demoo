Data Science with Healthcare

In today's tech-driven world, access to prompt and accurate healthcare information is more crucial than ever...

The Challenge

Our project started with a fundamental realization: people frequently experience symptoms and seek rapid insights into their health concerns...

The Solution

We've crafted a web app that empowers users to enter a list of symptoms they are experiencing...

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through a user-friendly interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: Our Decision Tree Classifier predicts potential diseases.
  3. Prediction: The app provides instant disease predictions.
  4. Additional Information: Includes precautions, medications, and dietary advice.
  5. User Records: Users can store personal health data.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Clean and intuitive design.
  • Precise Predictions: AI-powered disease diagnosis.
  • Comprehensive Information: Detailed descriptions and medical advice.
  • Educational Content: Blog posts on health topics.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App represents a fusion of machine learning and healthcare...
